Bio

I am a postdoctoral researcher in economics at Carl von Ossietzky Universität Oldenburg.

My research interests include applied development economics, environmental economics, and energy economics.

I examine how institutions, climate change, energy policies, and development interventions affect poverty, education, and well-being at the individual and household levels. Methodologically, I use applied econometric methods to address policy-relevant questions within these domains.
